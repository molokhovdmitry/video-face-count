Sebastian Hans Eli Fors (born 16 December 1990), known by the pseudonym Forsen, is a Swedish Twitch streamer who is known for competing in Starcraft II and Hearthstone. He is also known for his rowdy fanbase, who call themselves "Forsenboys" and have had a hand in popularizing a number of controversial internet memes. In January 2018, a warped image of Fors' face ("forsenE") became the most-used emote on Twitch worldwide. As of April 2020, he is the 33rd largest streamer on Twitch by subscriber count.
